Big Town Contracting & Fab LLC is a Nashville-based contracting and fabrication company specializing in ornamental welding, custom metalwork, and general contracting.

From custom railings and ornamental iron to fencing, siding, and structural fabrication, we bring precision and pride to every project. Whether it's residential or commercial, no job is too big or too small.

What We Do

Ornamental Welding

Custom ornamental ironwork including decorative railings, gates, window guards, and architectural metalwork that adds beauty and value to your property.

Custom Fabrication

Precision metal fabrication for structural components, brackets, custom fixtures, and specialty pieces. We work with steel, aluminum, and more.

General Contracting

Full-service contracting including fencing, siding, exterior improvements, and property upgrades for residential and commercial projects.

Stairs & Railings

Custom-built metal staircases, handrails, and balustrades designed for safety, durability, and visual impact.

Fencing

Wood, metal, and ornamental fencing solutions for privacy, security, and curb appeal. Custom designs to match your property's style.

Siding & Exteriors

Professional siding installation, shutters, and exterior cladding to protect and beautify your home or commercial building.

Custom Furniture

One-of-a-kind tables, benches, and furniture pieces combining live edge wood with custom-welded metal bases and frames.
